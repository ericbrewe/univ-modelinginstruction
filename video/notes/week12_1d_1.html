In this clip, the students are at their desk before their exam begins while the professor is addressing them. The professor explains the rules of the exam including no bags on the desk, and to be quiet and mindful of the other groups. The professor explains the allotted time, and how the cheating policy is strictly enforced. The professor then asks students to turn their cell phones off and not to text or answer calls during the exam, and allows students to use scrap paper and stand up if they desire. At the end of the clip, the professor begins the exam.

This clip takes place during week 12 (Practicing with Forces & Exam).
